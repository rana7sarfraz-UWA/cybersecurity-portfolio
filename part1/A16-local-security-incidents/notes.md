# A16 – Local Security Incidents

Several cybersecurity incidents have occurred in Australia.

1. Optus Data Breach (2022)
A major telecommunications company suffered a breach exposing customer data.

2. Medibank Data Breach (2022)
Sensitive health information was leaked after a ransomware attack.

3. Latitude Financial Data Breach (2023)
Millions of personal records were stolen from the financial services company.

These incidents highlight the importance of strong data protection and security policies.
