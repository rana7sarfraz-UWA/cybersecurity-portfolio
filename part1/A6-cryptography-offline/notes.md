# A6 – Cryptography Used Offline

Modern devices use encryption to protect stored data.

Example: Disk Encryption

Windows uses BitLocker to encrypt the entire hard drive. This ensures that if a device is stolen, the attacker cannot access the stored data without the encryption key.

Smartphones also use built-in encryption to protect files, photos and personal information.

Offline encryption protects data even when the device is not connected to the internet.
