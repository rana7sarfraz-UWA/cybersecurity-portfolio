# A17 – Types of Locks

Locks provide physical security by preventing unauthorized entry.

Examples include:

1. Padlock
2. Deadbolt lock
3. Knob lock
4. Lever handle lock
5. Smart lock
6. Combination lock
7. Electronic keypad lock
8. Bicycle lock
9. Magnetic lock
10. Cam lock

These locks are commonly used in homes, offices, and public areas.
