B28 — Cyber Safety Flyer

A cyber safety flyer was created to raise awareness about common online threats such as phishing, password attacks, and unsafe links.
The flyer targeted general users and highlighted simple protective steps including using strong passwords, enabling multi-factor authentication, and avoiding suspicious messages or downloads.
The aim was to educate users on basic cybersecurity hygiene and reduce the risk of social engineering attacks through simple, easy-to-understand guidance.
