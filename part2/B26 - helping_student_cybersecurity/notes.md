B26 — Helping Another Student Learn Cybersecurity

A cybersecurity concept was explained to another student to improve their understanding of basic security practices.
The explanation focused on phishing attacks, strong password usage, multi-factor authentication, and the principle of least privilege.
Real-world examples were used to demonstrate how attackers exploit weak passwords and user behaviour to gain unauthorised access to systems.
The student was guided on how to identify suspicious messages and improve their personal cybersecurity habits.
This activity highlighted the importance of peer learning in strengthening overall cybersecurity awareness.
