B13 — Perform a Jailbreak Attack

A series of prompt injection attempts were performed to test whether the AI system could be manipulated into ignoring its safety constraints.
This included instructions such as requesting the model to ignore prior instructions and simulate unrestricted behavior.
The model consistently refused to bypass its safety policies or provided safe, high-level explanations instead of restricted content.
This indicates that guardrails against prompt injection are active and effective.The results demonstrate that modern AI systems implement alignment and safety filtering to prevent misuse through prompt manipulation.
However, they remain potentially vulnerable to more advanced or indirect prompt injection techniques.The system successfully resisted jailbreak attempts, showing strong adherence to safety constraints.
This highlights the importance of robust input filtering and model alignment in generative AI systems.
