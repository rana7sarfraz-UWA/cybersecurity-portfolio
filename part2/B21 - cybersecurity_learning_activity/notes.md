B21 — Cybersecurity Learning Activity

A short cybersecurity learning activity was designed to help users identify phishing emails and unsafe online behaviour. 
The activity included examples of suspicious links, fake login pages, and common phishing tactics used in emails and messages.
Participants were asked to identify which examples were legitimate and which were malicious. 
The activity helped improve awareness of phishing attacks and safe browsing practices.
The activity demonstrated how interactive learning can improve cybersecurity awareness and reduce the risk of social engineering attacks.
