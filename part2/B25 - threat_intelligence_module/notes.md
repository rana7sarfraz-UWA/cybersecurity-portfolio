B25 — Threat Intelligence Module

A simple threat intelligence module was designed to monitor and identify suspicious login activity from system logs.
The module analysed authentication logs for repeated failed login attempts which may indicate brute-force attack behaviour.
Basic detection rules were implemented to flag suspicious activity and generate alerts when multiple failed logins were detected within a short period of time.
This demonstrated how threat intelligence and log monitoring can improve early detection of malicious behaviour and support proactive cybersecurity defence.
