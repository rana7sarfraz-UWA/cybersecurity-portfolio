B16 — Survey of Cybersecurity Solutions

A short comparison was conducted between modern cybersecurity tools including Microsoft Defender, CrowdStrike, and SentinelOne.
Microsoft Defender provides basic built-in protection for everyday users, CrowdStrike offers advanced enterprise-level threat detection and monitoring, while SentinelOne uses AI-based automated response to detect and stop threats.
These solutions show different levels of security capability depending on use case and environment.
Overall, enterprise solutions provide stronger protection due to real-time monitoring and advanced threat intelligence.
